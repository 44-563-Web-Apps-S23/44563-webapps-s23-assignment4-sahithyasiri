TRADITION George Middleton

OLLIVANT

[_Affectionately._]

Glad to be home again, Mary?

MARY

[_Evasively._]

The garden is so pretty.

EMILY

[_Softly, as she looks at_ MARY.]

I think I understand.

OLLIVANT

Hasn't changed much, eh?

MARY

It seems different; perhaps it's the night.

OLLIVANT

I guess it isn't up to its usual standard. Haven't seen your mother there so often this spring.

SIR HARRY

[_Complacently._]

Did that seem all right, eh?

LADY SIMS

[_Much relieved._]

I think perfect.

TOMBES

[_Unbending._]

Especially the females, Sir Harry.
